### Retail & Visual (Boutiques, Salons)
- **Hook (The Silent Slip):** "70% of your first-time customers never come back. Not because they hated the service, but because life got busy. Stop the silent slip with automated follow-ups."
- **Hook (The ROI Calculator):** "What's a repeat customer worth to you? If one customer visits 3x more per year, that's hundreds in extra revenue. Let CCM do the work."

### Medical & High-Frequency (Dental, Optical)
- **Hook (The Benefit Reset):** "Patients forget their benefits reset. Don't let their health (and your revenue) expire. Automate your year-end outreach."
- **Hook (Recall Gap):** "Patients who skip 6 months often wait 18. Break the cycle and keep your chairs full on autopilot."

### General Small Biz
- **Hook (The 'Too Busy'):** "You're too busy to be a marketer. You should be running your business. Let our AI handle the retention while you handle the customers."
