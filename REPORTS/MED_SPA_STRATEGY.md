## 🎨 Brand Voice Notes for Med Spa Outreach

- **Tone:** Professional but warm. Medical aesthetics is a blend of clinical expertise + personal care. Mirror that.
- **Empathy Language:** "You pour your heart into every treatment" / "You earned their trust"
- **Financial Hooks:** Lead with the CAC math — Med Spa owners understand acquisition cost numbers intimately
- **Avoid:** Overly aggressive sales language. Med Spa owners are healthcare-adjacent professionals who value discretion.
- **Connection Guard Framing:** Position automation as "care in action" — not a sales pitch but a way to show clients you're still thinking about them.
