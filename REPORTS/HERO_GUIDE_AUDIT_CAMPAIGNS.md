# Hero-Guide Audit: 15-Niche Social Ad Campaigns

This report outlines the refined ad copy for all 15 niches, audited through the Donald Miller Hero-Guide framework. 

## The Framework
1. **The Hero**: The Local Business Owner (overwhelmed, losing customers).
2. **The Guide**: Customer Comeback Machine (empathy for the "too busy" owner, authority via automation).
3. **The Problem**: Churn, "Leaky Bucket" revenue, and the high cost of acquisition.
4. **The Plan**: Simple 3-step integration (QR Code -> Automation -> Repeat Revenue).
5. **The CTA**: Clear, direct "Founding Member" offer.

---

## 1. Med Spas (Botox/Fillers)
- **Problem**: Paying $300 to acquire a patient just to lose them to the competitor's "New Patient Special" after 90 days.
- **Hero-Guide Hook**: "You worked hard to get that patient. Don't let a competitor's discount take them away."
- **Ad Copy**: Stop the Bleeding. Most Botox patients need a refresh every 90 days, but life gets in the way. If you aren't in their inbox when they look in the mirror, they're gone. CCM automates the 'Refresh' reminder so they book their next visit before they even leave your chair.
- **Offer**: Founding Member Rate: $49/mo.

## 2. Dental Practices (Invisalign/Hygiene)
- **Problem**: High-margin hygiene slots going empty while the front desk wastes hours on manual chase-calls.
- **Hero-Guide Hook**: "Fill your hygiene slots on autopilot. Stop the manual chase."
- **Ad Copy**: Your front desk is for greeting patients, not for begging them to come back. CCM automates the re-engagement loop for Invisalign and Hygiene patients with a simple QR scan at checkout. Keep your chairs full and your team focused on care.

## 3. Optical Shops (Style & Sight)
- **Problem**: Patients forget their benefits reset, leading to expired revenue and lost frame sales.
- **Hero-Guide Hook**: "Benefits expire. Your revenue shouldn't."
- **Ad Copy**: Life gets busy, and even loyal patients forget their annual eye exams. When their benefits expire, so does your opportunity for a frame sale. CCM automates the 'Style & Sight' refresh, inviting them back before their insurance resets.

## 4. Boutiques (VIP Win-back)
- **Problem**: 70% of first-time shoppers never return.
- **Hero-Guide Hook**: "Turn one-time shoppers into lifelong regulars."
- **Ad Copy**: Not every visitor is a regular yet. CCM turns that first scan at the rack into a VIP win-back sequence. Don't let your best inventory sit on the shelf—bring them back with automated 'New Arrival' nudges tailored to their style.

## 5. Salons (Loyalty Loops)
- **Problem**: The "6-week drift"—when a client loves their cut but forgets to re-book until their roots show (or they go elsewhere).
- **Hero-Guide Hook**: "Lock in your chair's ROI."
- **Ad Copy**: Your talent keeps them coming back, but your schedule needs a 'Machine' to keep it full. CCM automates re-booking reminders the second they leave the salon, ensuring they stay in *your* chair, not the one down the street.

## 6. Pet Groomers (Re-booking)
- **Problem**: Hands-on owners have zero time for CRM; mats happen, and clients drift.
- **Hero-Guide Hook**: "The $49/mo assistant your grooming shop needs."
- **Ad Copy**: You're busy with the pups. You shouldn't be busy with the phone. CCM handles the '6-week Fluff Follow-up' automatically. When a pup gets a trim, the machine starts the clock. Your calendar fills up while you're grooming.

## 7. Veterinary Clinics (Preventative Care)
- **Problem**: Losing pharmacy and wellness revenue to online giants like Chewy.
- **Hero-Guide Hook**: "Protect your practice's preventative revenue."
- **Ad Copy**: Don't let 'Online Drift' take your wellness revenue. CCM automates the re-engagement for annual exams and med refills, proving your value to pet owners before they look elsewhere.

## 8. Coffee Shops (Frequency Boost)
- **Problem**: The "Morning Routine" is fragile; one missed day becomes a new habit at a competitor.
- **Hero-Guide Hook**: "Become their unshakeable morning routine."
- **Ad Copy**: A loyalty card in a wallet is easily forgotten. A nudge on a phone is not. CCM boosts visit frequency by automating afternoon 'pick-me-up' offers to your morning regulars.

## 9. Pest Control (Invisible Service)
- **Problem**: Customers think 'no bugs' means the service isn't needed.
- **Hero-Guide Hook**: "Prove your value before they cancel."
- **Ad Copy**: Your best work is invisible. That’s the problem. CCM automates 'Service Impact Reports' so your customers see the bugs you *prevented*, keeping your recurring contracts safe and your revenue steady.

## 10. Pool Maintenance (Recurring ROI)
- **Problem**: Clear water makes customers think they can do it themselves.
- **Hero-Guide Hook**: "ROI Protection for your recurring contracts."
- **Ad Copy**: When the pool is blue, the customer thinks they don't need you. CCM automates 'Water Health Reports' and 'Upcoming Prevention' hooks, so they know exactly what they're paying for—peace of mind.

## 11. Personal Training (The Guilt Gap)
- **Problem**: One missed session leads to 'guilt drift' and eventual cancellation.
- **Hero-Guide Hook**: "Bridge the 'Guilt Gap' before they quit."
- **Ad Copy**: When a client misses a session, they don't feel empowered; they feel guilty. And guilt leads to cancellations. CCM automates the 'Accountability Check' the second they miss a class, bringing them back before the drift becomes permanent.

## 12. Commercial Cleaning (Contract Protection)
- **Problem**: Facility managers switch for a $10 difference because the relationship feels transactional.
- **Hero-Guide Hook**: "Build a relationship that beats a low-ball offer."
- **Ad Copy**: Don't let your contract be decided by a flyer on a desk. CCM automates proactive quality surveys and appreciation loops, making you the 'Guide' they can't afford to lose.

## 13. Dry Cleaners (Frequency Nudges)
- **Problem**: Missing the 'usual drop-off' day is the first step to losing a regular.
- **Hero-Guide Hook**: "Wardrobe Insurance for your regulars."
- **Ad Copy**: If they miss their Tuesday drop-off, they might be trying the place next to their office. CCM automates 'Miss You' incentives the moment their pattern breaks, keeping their wardrobe—and your revenue—in your shop.

## 14. Physiotherapy (Recovery Compliance)
- **Problem**: Patients stop coming when they feel 'fine,' leading to relapse.
- **Hero-Guide Hook**: "Turn 'Fine' into 'Finished Recovery'."
- **Ad Copy**: The hardest part of recovery is the last 10%. Patients drift when the pain stops, but before the healing is done. CCM automates 'Progress Checks' and 'Maintenance' reminders to ensure they finish the plan and stay healthy.

## 15. Lawn Care (Seasonal Prep)
- **Problem**: Losing summer regulars to the neighborhood kid with a cheaper flyer in March.
- **Hero-Guide Hook**: "Lock in 12-month loyalty before the grass grows."
- **Ad Copy**: Don't wait for the first mow to secure your season. CCM automates 'Spring Re-activation' and 'Fall Prep' campaigns to your past clients, ensuring your schedule is locked in months in advance.
