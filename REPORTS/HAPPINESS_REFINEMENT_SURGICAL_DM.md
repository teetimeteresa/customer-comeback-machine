## Key Refinements Summary:
1.  **Lead with the "Heart":** Acknowledge their craft or their brand name first.
2.  **Use the "Reason to Come Home" Hook:** This positions us as a bridge back to the business.
3.  **The "Warm Hug" Reassurance:** Positions automation as a caring gesture, not a robotic system.
4.  **Community Focus:** Mention the specific city to make it feel neighborly.
