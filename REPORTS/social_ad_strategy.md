# Social Ad Strategy Brief: Customer Comeback Machine

## 1. Platform Strategy & Targeting

### Primary: Facebook & Instagram (Meta)
*   **Ideal For:** Boutiques, Salons, Coffee Shops, and Med Spas.
*   **Why:** These are highly visual businesses. Owners are active on Meta platforms for both personal and business use.
*   **Targeting Strategy:**
    *   **Interests:** "Small Business Owner," "Retail Management," "Salon Owners," "Medical Practice Management."
    *   **Behaviors:** "Facebook Page Admins (Business)," "Small business owners."
    *   **Lookalikes:** Upload current customer lists (once we have >100) to find similar "busy local owners."

### Secondary: LinkedIn
*   **Ideal For:** Medical Practices (Dental, Optical), Veterinary Clinics.
*   **Why:** Higher perceived professionalism. Practice managers and doctors are more reachable via professional job titles.
*   **Targeting Strategy:**
    *   **Job Titles:** "Practice Manager," "Owner," "Dentist," "Optometrist," "Veterinarian."
    *   **Groups:** "Medical Group Management Association," "Dental Practice Management."

### Emerging: TikTok
*   **Ideal For:** "Cool" Salons, Specialty Retail, and Modern Coffee Shops.
*   **Content Type:** Educational, fast-paced "A-Ha!" moments about customer churn.
*   **Hook:** "Why your best customers are leaving (and how to stop them in 60 seconds)."

---

## 2. High-Performing Ad Hooks (By Niche)

### Visual/Retail (Boutiques, Salons)
*   **Hook 1 (The Silent Slip):** "70% of your first-time customers never come back. Not because they hated the service, but because life got busy. Stop the silent slip with automated follow-ups."
*   **Hook 2 (The ROI Calculator):** "What's a repeat customer worth to you? If one customer visits 3x more per year, that's [Amount] extra revenue. Let Customer Comeback Machine do the work for you."

### Medical/High-Frequency (Dental, Optical, Veterinary)
*   **Hook 3 (The Benefit Reset):** "Patients forget their benefits reset. Don't let their eye health (and your revenue) expire. Automate your year-end outreach."
*   **Hook 4 (The Booster Gap):** "Losing pharmacy revenue to Chewy? Bring pet owners back into your clinic for wellness exams and meds on autopilot."

### General Small Biz
*   **Hook 5 (The 'Too Busy' Hook):** "You're too busy to be a marketer. You should be running your business. Let our AI 'Machine' handle the retention while you handle the customers."

---

## 3. 'Starving Niches' (Underserved & High Churn)

These niches have high customer lifetime value (LTV) but suffer from low automated engagement:

1.  **Med Spas (Botox/Fillers):** Treatments expire every 3-4 months. Patients often drift to whoever has a "new patient special." CCM can lock them in with "refresh" reminders.
2.  **Pet Groomers:** High frequency (every 4-8 weeks). Groomers are usually "hands-on" and have zero time for CRM management.
3.  **Chiropractors:** Patients stop coming when they feel "fine," but need maintenance to stay well. CRM reminders for "alignment checks" are underserved.
4.  **Auto Detailers:** High LTV, seasonal demand. Needs "seasonal salt-rinse" or "spring-clean" hooks.
5.  **Specialty Bakeries (Custom Cakes):** Transition from a "one-time wedding/birthday cake" to "anniversary/every-birthday" regular.
