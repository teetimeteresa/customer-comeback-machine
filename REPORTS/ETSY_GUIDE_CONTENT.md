### Page 3: The Hero-Guide Framework for Customer Comebacks
To bring them back, you need a message that cuts through the noise. We use a proven Narrative Marketing framework:

1.  **The Hero:** Your customer. They are busy, overwhelmed, and looking for a solution (or a treat!).
2.  **The Guide:** YOU. You have the empathy to understand their busy life and the authority to help them (with your service/product).
3.  **The Problem:** Churn and drift. They want to come back, but they need a nudge.
4.  **The Plan:** A simple 3-step loop: Scan -> Connect -> Return.
5.  **The CTA:** A direct invitation to book their next visit or claim a VIP reward.

**Example (The Med Spa):** "You worked hard for that glow. Don't let it fade. Book your 90-day refresh now."

### Page 4: Implementation — The 3-Step Success Plan
How do you actually "Stop the Bleeding"? You need a machine.

1.  **The Digital Handshake:** Use a QR code at your checkout or on your tables. Offer something small but meaningful (a birthday treat, a recovery tip, a VIP alert).
2.  **The Automated Nudge:** Set up a sequence that triggers after they leave. 
    *   *Day 1:* A "Warm Hug" Thank You.
    *   *Day 21:* The "Sharp Edge" Reminder.
    *   *Day 45:* The "We Miss You" Win-back.
3.  **The Frequency Boost:** Use seasonal batch alerts to announce fresh products or new openings.

**Sector Spotlight (The Bakery):** Use a "Fresh Batch" alert to tell your regulars when the sourdough is coming out of the oven. It turns a "maybe" into a "must-go" visit.
