# Niche-Specific "Success Session" Video Scripts
## For Sales Closer — 5-Minute Personalized Loom/Video Demo

**Tone:** Warm, supportive, non-salesy. "I" and "You" language. Like a partner, not a pitch.
**Framework:** StoryBrand SB7 (Hero → Problem → Guide → Plan → CTA)
**Core Math:** "One recovered customer pays for the year."
**CTA:** Founding Member link ($49/mo, setup waived, 10 spots per niche)

---

## 🎬 General Video Instructions (All Niches)

- **Camera:** Look into the lens. Not at the screen.
- **Background:** Blurred image of YOUR desktop/Loom background, OR a screenshot of the prospect's Google Maps/website.
- **Props:** Have the CCM Owner HQ Mobile App open on your phone (or a mockup) to show during the demo section. Have a sample QR code sign ready.
- **Tone:** You're a consultant, not a salesperson. You're showing them a problem they didn't know they had — and an elegant solution.
- **Length:** Aim for 4-5 minutes. Don't rush. Pauses are powerful.
- **Ending:** Direct them to the Founding Member link in the video description. Personalized link preferred.
