## What Is CCM?

Customer Comeback Machine turns one-time visitors into repeat customers. Members scan a QR code at checkout, and CCM automatically sends:
- ✅ **Thank-you messages** after every visit
- ✅ **Review requests** to build their online reputation
- ✅ **Re-booking reminders** to bring customers back
- ✅ **Win-back campaigns** for customers who haven't visited
- ✅ **Birthday club & referral rewards**

All automated. No time required from the business owner.
