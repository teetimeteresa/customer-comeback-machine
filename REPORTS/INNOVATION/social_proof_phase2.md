## 🦷 Graphic 1: Cosmetic Dentistry — "The $10k Patient Retention Gap"

### Title/Headline
The $10k Patient Retention Gap — Closed by a QR Code

### Subtitle
Your highest-value patients are one forgotten appointment away from never coming back.

**Bottom bar (spans full width):**
- A ribbon in amber-to-rose gradient
- Text: "One $10k patient retained = 17 years of CCM. ($588/yr vs $10k LTV)"
- CCM logo, bottom right

| Metric | Value |

- **Format:** 1080×1080px (square for social) / 1200×628px (LinkedIn header)
- **Iconography:** Dental chair silhouette, calendar icon, QR code with dental cross, shield icon (Connection Guard)
- **Emotional tone:** High-end, professional, warm. Cosmetic dentistry is premium — reflect that.

## 🦴 Graphic 3: Chiropractic — "Patient Compliance: Automated Nudges for Better Recovery"

### Title/Headline
The "Pain-Only" Cycle Ends Here — Keep Patients on the Path to Wellness

### Subtitle
60% of chiropractic patients ghost after 3 visits. Not because they're healed — because they feel 'fine enough.'

---

### Visual Concept

**Three-Panel Journey Design:**

**Panel 1 (The Problem — left, slate-gray/blue):**
- A silhouette of a person walking away from a chiropractic table, looking back with a shrug
- A calendar showing "Visit 1 ✅ • Visit 2 ✅ • Visit 3 ✅" then a blank gap
- Text: *"60% churn after 3 visits. The pain stops. So do they."*
- Small dollar signs fading into the distance

**Panel 2 (The Bridge — center, amber transition):**
- A smartphone screen showing an automated CCM text: *"Hey {Name}, it's been 3 weeks since your last adjustment. How's that lower back feeling? Ready to lock in the progress? Book your next alignment here →"*
- Small QR code graphic
- Text: *"CCM bridges the 'Guilt Gap' — automated check-ins that feel like care, not sales."*

**Panel 3 (The Result — right, warm rose/amber):**
- A full chiropractic room with a smiling patient on the table
- A calendar showing "Month 1 ✅ • Month 2 ✅ • Month 3 ✅ • Month 4 ✅" — consistent visits
- Text: *"Patients complete their recovery plan. Revenue stabilizes. Referrals multiply."*

**Bottom ribbon:**
- "One recovered patient ($1,200-$2,400/yr in wellness visits) pays for 2-4 YEARS of CCM."

---

### Supporting Copy

> *"Dr. Rivera was frustrated. She was getting great results with her patients — but 6 out of 10 wouldn't come back after the initial 3-visit package.*
>
> *Not because she failed them. Because they felt 'good enough' and life got in the way.*
>
> *CCM automated her wellness check-ins. After each adjustment, a warm SMS at 2 weeks: 'How's that alignment feeling?' At 4 weeks: 'Ready for your next tune-up? Your body will thank you.'*
>
> *Patient compliance jumped 40%. One maintenance patient ($1,800/year) paid for 3 years of CCM.*
>
> *The other 39 retained patients? Pure practice growth."*

---

### Data Points to Feature

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Chiro patient churn after 3 visits | 60% |
| Initial visit package | $300-$600 (3 visits) |
| Monthly maintenance value | $100-$200/visit |
| Annual wellness patient LTV | $1,200-$2,400 |
| CCM cost vs. one maintenance patient | $588/yr vs $1,200-$2,400/yr |

---

### Design Specs

- **Palette:** Slate-blue (#475569) for "Drift" panel → Amber (#F59E0B) for "Bridge" → Rose (#E11D48) for "Result"
- **Typography:** Clean, health-professional sans-serif
- **Format:** 1200×628px (three-panel landscape works best for LinkedIn) / 1080×1080px (vertical stack for Instagram)
- **Iconography:** Spine/body silhouette, calendar with checkmarks, smartphone with SMS notification, QR code
- **Emotional tone:** Professional, health-centered, empathetic. Chiropractors are doctors — match that authority while keeping warmth.
