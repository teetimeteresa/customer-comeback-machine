# Instagram DM Templates — For Sales Closer

## COMPLIANCE RULES (Read First)
1. **Be transparent** — Identify yourself as Customer Comeback Machine. Don't pretend to be "just researching."
2. **MAX 2 DMs per prospect** — If no reply after DM2, STOP. Silence is not consent.
3. **Easy opt-out** — Every DM must include or imply an easy way to say "no."
4. **Respect opt-outs** — If someone says "not interested" or "stop," thank them immediately and never contact them again.
5. **No bulk automation** — Every DM must be personalized. Never copy-paste the same message to multiple people.
6. **No scraping private data** — Only use public Instagram profiles (public bios, public follower lists).

## Opt-Out Procedure

When a prospect says any of the following:
- "Not interested"
- "Stop"
- "Don't contact me"
- "No thanks"
- Or any clear indication they don't want to be contacted

**Do this immediately:**
1. Reply with: *"No problem at all, {name}! Thanks for letting me know — I won't message again. Wishing you all the best with {business}! 🙌"*
2. Log the opt-out in the lead database (set `status = 'opted_out'` and `optOutDate = today`)
3. **Never contact them again** — not via DM, email, or any other channel

## Pro Tips

1. **Personalize the first line.** Reference their recent post: "Loved your post about {topic}!"
2. **Identify yourself.** Say "I run Customer Comeback Machine" — not "I'm researching."
3. **Never pitch in DM 1.** First DM is a genuine question.
4. **Two messages max.** If you send DM2 and they don't reply, that's your answer. Move on.
5. **Send during business hours.** Tue-Thu 10am-2pm in their timezone works best.
6. **Keep DMs to 2-3 sentences.** Instagram is not a newsletter.
7. **Track everything.** Log: contacted date, replied, opted_out, converted. If they opt out, HONOR IT.
