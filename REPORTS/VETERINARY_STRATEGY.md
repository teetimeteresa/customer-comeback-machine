## 🎨 Brand Voice Notes for Veterinary Outreach

- **Tone:** Warm, compassionate, professional. Veterinary is a unique blend of medical authority and deep emotional connection. Respect both.
- **Empathy Language:** "You became a vet to care for animals" / "Your patients can't tell you when they need care. Your system can."
- **Financial Hooks:** Lead with the "Chewy problem" — vets know this pain intimately and it's top-of-mind.
- **Emotional Hooks:** Frame everything around the pet. The client's dog/cat is the real "customer" — the owner is the decision-maker.
- **Connection Guard Framing:** Position automation as "extending your care between visits" — clients feel like you're always watching out for their pet.
- **Avoid:** Overly aggressive sales language. Veterinary professionals are caregivers first, business owners second.
