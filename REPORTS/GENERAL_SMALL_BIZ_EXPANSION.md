# Strategic Expansion: General Small Business Niches

This report identifies 5 high-potential niches for expanding the Customer Comeback Machine beyond its initial focus on Dental and MedSpas.

## 1. Top 5 New Niches & Retention Pain Points

### A. Pet Groomers (High Retention Potential)
*   **The "Bleeding" Pain Point**: The 6-8 week "Cycle Gap." Customers often forget to book the next appointment until the pet is overgrown, leading to irregular revenue and lost opportunities to upsell treatments.
*   **The CCM Hook**: Automated "Your pup is due for a refresh" SMS/Email 6 weeks after the last scan.

### B. Auto Detailers / Specialty Repair Shops
*   **The "Bleeding" Pain Point**: One-off "emergency" or "event" visits. Owners lack a system to remind customers about preventative maintenance or seasonal detailing.
*   **The CCM Hook**: "Keep your car showroom ready" – seasonal detailing reminders and automated review requests to build trust in a skeptical industry.

### C. Boutique Coffee Shops
*   **The "Bleeding" Pain Point**: The "One-Timer" commuter. High foot traffic but low capture rate of customer data compared to major chains (Starbucks/Dunkin).
*   **The CCM Hook**: "Skip the line & earn a treat" – scan QR to join the loyalty club, triggering a "See you tomorrow?" discount if they don't return within 48 hours.

### D. Artisan Bakeries
*   **The "Bleeding" Pain Point**: Weekend-only customers. High weekday wastage and missing out on "special occasion" (birthdays/anniversaries) cake orders.
*   **The CCM Hook**: Birthday club automation. Capture the scan for a free croissant, then automate a "Book your birthday cake" reminder 3 weeks before their big day.

### E. Yoga / Pilates Studios (Independent)
*   **The "Bleeding" Pain Point**: The "First-Class Churn." New students take one introductory class and never return.
*   **The CCM Hook**: Automated "How was your first flow?" survey + a "3-class pack" special offer sent 4 hours after their QR check-in.

The Growth Hacker should prioritize scraping businesses in the following high-growth, affluent markets:

2.  **Scottsdale, AZ**: High LTV service market (Pet grooming/Auto detailing).
3.  **Nashville, TN**: Rapidly growing retail and hospitality sector.
4.  **Denver, CO**: Strong boutique and outdoor lifestyle shop presence.
5.  **Charlotte, NC**: Established service-based economy with high retention needs.
