---

**Happiness Refinement:**
**Subject:** We're so glad you're here, {First Name}! ❤️ {Business Name} is in good hands.

**Revised Body:**
Hi {First Name},

Welcome to the family! We’ve been watching what you’re building at {Business Name}, and honestly? We’re fans. 

Our goal isn’t just to give you software—it’s to give you your time back and make your customers feel as special as they are. Here is the simple plan to get your 'Comeback Machine' humming:

1. **The 'Love at First Sight' Sign** — Our design team is already working on a custom-branded QR sign just for you. It’ll look beautiful on your counter.
2. **The Connection** — You place the sign, and we handle the rest. No tech headaches, no extra work for your team.
3. **The Ripple Effect** — Automated thank-yous, birthday wishes, and 'thought of you' notes start flowing. Your regulars feel seen, and they come back more often.

**You focus on what you love (serving your customers). We’ll focus on bringing them back.**

I’m sending your AI-Guided Onboarding link now. Reply "ready" and let’s start this journey together!

Warmly,
{Name}

## The "Happiness Lead" Golden Rules for Outreach:
1.  **Use "We/Us" to show partnership.** We are in this *with* them.
2.  **Celebrate their business.** Every message should contain a genuine compliment (if possible).
3.  **Low Friction, High Love.** Always offer to do the heavy lifting.
4.  **No Deadlines, Only Opportunities.** Replace "Offer expires in 24h" with "We're holding this special spot for you."
