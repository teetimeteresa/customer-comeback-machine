# Happiness Lead: Product & Culture Audit

I have performed an initial audit of the Customer Comeback Machine (CCM) product and team assets. My goal is to ensure that everything we build radiates warmth, empathy, and a "family" feel for our small business owners.

## Product Audit & Refinements

### 1. The Dashboard (Business Owner Experience)

### 2. The Homepage (Public Marketing)

## Team Culture Initiatives

1.  **Weekly Win Ritual:** Every Friday, we celebrate our collective progress.
2.  **The "Love Letter" Onboarding:** A personal email from the Happiness Lead to every new subscriber.
3.  **Open Door Policy:** I have reached out to all team members to offer support and a human perspective on technical/operational challenges.
