## 5-Email Sequence

### Email 1: Fresh Baked Welcome (Day 0 — Thank You)
**Subject:** Thanks for stopping by! 🥐
> "Hi {Name}, thanks for visiting {Bakery} today! We hope everything was perfectly baked and absolutely delicious. Everything we make is crafted with love, and knowing it made someone's day — that's why we do what we do. Come back soon for something sweet (or savory)!"

### Email 2: Share a Bite (Day 2 — Review Request)
**Subject:** How was that first bite? 🥐
> "Hi {Name}, we'd love to know what you thought! If you enjoyed your treat, would you mind sharing a photo and a few words on Google? It helps other food lovers find us, and it truly makes our bakers' day! [Share your experience →]"

### Email 3: Something Sweet (Day 7 — Comeback Offer)
**Subject:** We saved a treat for you 🥐
> "Hi {Name}, we've been baking up something new and we think you'd love it! Here's {offer} off your next visit. Whether you're craving something flaky, chocolatey, or perfectly crusty — we've got you covered."

### Email 4: Share the Loaf (Day 21 — Referral Request)
**Subject:** Know someone who loves great bread?
> "Hi {Name}, the best things in life are meant to be shared — especially great food! If you know someone who'd appreciate our baking, send them our way. When you refer a fellow food lover, {referral offer}. Because everything tastes better when shared!"

### Email 5: Seasonal Craving (Day 45 — Win-Back)
**Subject:** Something {seasonal} just came out of the oven...
> "Hi {Name}, it's been a little while since your last visit, and we've got some amazing {seasonal} specials that just came out of the oven. Think {example seasonal item}. We'd love to see you again! Come taste what's new. 🥐"

## 💖 Happiness & Soul (Surprise & Delight)
*Goal: To make your bakery feel like their "Daily Loaf" connection.*

### 1. The "Warm Loaf" Alert
- **Action:** Automated text when a fresh batch is pulled (Manual Trigger by owner).
- **Copy:** *"Hi {Name}, {Owner Name} from {Business Name} here. We just pulled a fresh batch of {Item} out of the oven and the shop smells amazing! If you're nearby, come grab one while it's still warm. I'll save one behind the counter for the first 5 people who reply to this! 🥖🔥"*

### 2. The "Recipe Secrets" Gift
- **Action:** Automated email 24 hours after their first visit.
- **Copy:** *"We loved meeting you today, {Name}! We believe great bread is for everyone. Here’s a little secret: the trick to our {Item} is [Simple Tip]. We’d love to see you back soon to compare notes! 🥐🏡"*

### 3. The "Community Crust" Spotlight
- **Action:** Automated email monthly to top regulars.
- **Copy:** *"You're a vital part of our bakery family, {Name}. 🥐 We noticed you've been a regular lately and we just wanted to say thank you for supporting local craft. Next time you're in, the coffee is on us! ☕✨"*
