*Goal: To make every client feel like their car is your most important project.*

- **Action:** If photos were taken, trigger an email 4 hours after pickup.
- **Copy:** *"We couldn't help but stare at the transformation of your car today, {Name}. 😍 Check out this shot our team took—it's basically art. Feel free to share it (and tag us!) so everyone can see that showroom shine."*

### 2. The "Weather Warning" Concierge

- **Copy:** *"Hey {Name}, {Owner Name} from {Business Name} here. Just saw a storm is rolling in tomorrow. If you had a wax or coating recently, try to keep the car under cover if you can! We want to protect that finish as much as you do. 🌧️🛡️"*
