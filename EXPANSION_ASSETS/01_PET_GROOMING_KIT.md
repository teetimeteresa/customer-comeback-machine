## 5-Email Sequence

### Email 1: Fresh & Fabulous (Day 0 — Thank You)
**Subject:** Your pup is looking fabulous! 🐾
> "Hi {Name}, thanks for bringing {Pet Name} in for a grooming session today! They were such a good sport. We hope they're looking and feeling their absolute best. Remember to brush them every few days to keep that fresh-from-the-salon look lasting longer!"

### Email 2: Share the Love (Day 2 — Review Request)
**Subject:** Does {Pet Name} give your groomer 5 paws? 🐾
> "Hi {Name}, we hope {Pet Name} is still feeling fabulous! If you loved their groom, would you mind sharing a photo on Google? It helps other pet parents find us and gives us all the warm fuzzies! 🐶 [Share on Google →]"

### Email 3: Comeback Groom (Day 7 — Comeback Offer)
**Subject:** Time for a touch-up?
> "Hi {Name}, they say a well-groomed pup is a happy pup! Most of our furry friends come back every 4-6 weeks for a refresh. Here's {offer} off your next appointment — because {Pet Name} deserves to look their best year-round!"

### Email 4: Refer a Friend (Day 21 — Referral Request)
**Subject:** Know another fur baby who needs a spa day?
> "Hi {Name}, word of paw is the best way to grow! If you know another pet parent who would love our grooming services, send them our way. When you refer a friend, {referral offer}. Because every pup deserves the VIP treatment! 🐾"

### Email 5: We Miss You (Day 45 — Win-Back)
**Subject:** {Pet Name}'s groom is overdue! 🐾
> "Hi {Name}, it's been a little while since {Pet Name}'s last visit. We know life gets busy, but their coat might be getting a bit shaggy! We'd love to see them again for a refresh. Book here: [Link]"

## 💖 Happiness & Soul (Surprise & Delight)
*Goal: To make every pet parent feel like their fur baby is your favorite.*

### 1. The "Post-Bath Zoomies" Check-in
- **Action:** Automated text 3 hours after pickup.
- **Copy:** *"Hi {Name}, it's {Owner Name} from {Business Name}. Just wanted to check in—did {Pet Name} get the post-bath zoomies? 😂 We loved having them today. Give them an extra treat from us! 🐾"*

### 2. The "Style Icon" Spotlight
- **Action:** If a photo was taken during the groom, trigger an email 24h later.
- **Copy:** *"We’re still thinking about how cute {Pet Name} looked yesterday! They are a total style icon. If you post any photos, tag us—we’d love to show them off to the rest of the pack! 📸"*

### 3. The "Comfort First" Promise
- **Action:** Pre-visit email for high-anxiety pets.
- **Copy:** *"We know {Pet Name} can be a little nervous at the groomer. Don't worry—we’ve set aside extra 'cuddle time' for their appointment today to make sure they feel safe and loved. See you soon! ❤️"*
