## 5-Email Sequence

### Email 1: First-Time Glow (Day 0 — Thank You)
**Subject:** You did it! 🧘
> "Hi {Name}, thank you for joining us today! Taking that first step onto the mat is the hardest part — and you did great! We hope you're feeling a bit more centered, stronger, and proud of yourself. The glow you're feeling right now? That's your body saying 'thank you.' Come back soon!"

### Email 2: Keep the Flow (Day 2 — Review Request)
**Subject:** How was your practice?
> "Hi {Name}, we hope you're still feeling that post-class glow! If you enjoyed your experience, would you mind sharing it on Google? Your words help other people find the courage to step onto the mat for the first time. Namaste 🙏 [Share your experience →]"

### Email 3: Deepen Your Practice (Day 7 — Comeback Offer)
**Subject:** Your practice is calling 🧘
> "Hi {Name}, the second class is where the real magic happens. Your body starts to remember the movements, your breath finds its rhythm, and you begin to feel stronger. Here's {offer} on your next class or membership. Come flow with us again!"

### Email 4: Share the Mat (Day 21 — Referral Request)
**Subject:** Bring a friend to the mat 🧘
> "Hi {Name}, yoga and Pilates are better together! If you know someone who's been curious about starting their practice, bring them to a class. When you refer a friend, {referral offer}. Because everyone deserves to find their flow."

### Email 5: Come Back to Center (Day 45 — Win-Back)
**Subject:** Your mat is waiting for you 🧘
> "Hi {Name}, it's been a little while since we saw you in the studio. Life gets busy, we know. But your practice is always here for you — no judgment, just breath and movement. We'd love to see you back on the mat. Here's {offer} to welcome you back."

## 💖 Happiness & Soul (Surprise & Delight)
*Goal: To make every member feel like they belong to a sanctuary.*

### 1. The "First Milestone" Celebration
- **Action:** Automated email after their 5th class.
- **Copy:** *"You’re officially building a practice, {Name}! 🧘 We’ve loved watching your progress over the last 5 classes. We have a little something for you at the front desk next time you’re in—a CCM 'Inner Circle' sticker for your water bottle. Keep flowing! ✨"*

### 2. The "Wellness Check" (The Warm Hug)
- **Action:** Automated text if a member misses their usual class time by 48 hours.
- **Copy:** *"Hi {Name}, {Owner Name} from {Business Name} here. We missed your energy in class this week! Just wanted to send a quick 'Warm Hug' and remind you to take a deep breath today, even if you can't make it to the mat. We're here when you're ready! ❤️"*

### 3. The "Practice Partner" Referral
- **Action:** Automated email monthly.
- **Copy:** *"Practice is always better with a friend, {Name}. Who’s one person in your life who needs a little more 'Zen' right now? Send them this link for a free first class—on us. Let’s grow our sanctuary together. 🙏"*
