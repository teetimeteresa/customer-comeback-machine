## 5-Email Sequence

### Email 1: Perfect Brew (Day 0 — Thank You)
**Subject:** Thanks for stopping by! ☕
> "Hi {Name}, thanks for visiting {Shop} today! We hope you loved your brew. Whether you're a regular or a first-timer, we're so glad you chose to spend part of your day with us. Come back soon — there's always something new brewing!"

### Email 2: Share the Love (Day 2 — Review Request)
**Subject:** Rate that roast! ☕
> "Hi {Name}, if today's cup made you smile, would you mind sharing the love on Google? Reviews are like free coffee for our soul — they help other coffee lovers find their new favorite spot! [Share your experience →]"

### Email 3: Comeback Cup (Day 7 — Comeback Offer)
**Subject:** Your next cup is on us ☕
> "Hi {Name}, we know there are a million places to grab coffee. That's why we want to make sure your next visit is extra special. Here's {offer} off your next drink — on us. Come see what's new on our menu!"

### Email 4: Bring a Friend (Day 21 — Referral Request)
**Subject:** Good coffee is better shared ☕
> "Hi {Name}, the best coffee conversations happen with good company. If you know someone who'd love our shop, bring them in! When you refer a friend, {referral offer}. Because great coffee is even better when you share it."

### Email 5: We Miss You (Day 45 — Win-Back)
**Subject:** It's quieter in here without you ☕
> "Hi {Name}, we noticed it's been a little while since you stopped by. We've got some new roasts and seasonal specials we think you'd love. Come back and see us — your usual seat is waiting. See you soon?"

## 💖 Happiness & Soul (Surprise & Delight)
*Goal: To make your shop feel like their "Daily Home."*

### 1. The "Perfect Pour" Recognition
- **Action:** Automated text 1 hour after their 5th scan.
- **Copy:** *"Hi {Name}, we've noticed you've been coming in a lot lately! We love seeing your face at the counter. Next time you're in, ask for {Owner Name}—I'd love to say hi and give you a little 'regular's treat' on the house! ☕️"*

### 2. The "Rainy Day" Warm Hug
- **Action:** (Manual Trigger during bad weather) Automated email to the local list.
- **Copy:** *"It's a bit gloomy out there today, {Name}. 🌧️ We've got the heater on and the fresh beans grinding. Come take a break from the rain—your favorite corner is waiting. Mention 'Rainy Day' for a free cookie with your latte! 🍪"*

### 3. The "New Roast" First Look
- **Action:** Automated email to VIPs (10+ scans).
- **Copy:** *"Hey {Name}, we just got a small batch of a new Ethiopian roast that we think you'll love. We haven't put it on the main menu yet, but we're letting our Coffee Circle family try it first. Come in and ask for a 'First Look' pour! ☕️✨"*
