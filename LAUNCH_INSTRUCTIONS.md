# Customer Comeback Machine - Launch Instructions 🚀✨

Congratulations! You are now the owner of a fully automated SaaS business designed to help local businesses thrive.

## 1. Accounts You Need
- **Stripe**: For processing payments.
- **Resend**: For sending automated emails.
- **OpenAI**: To power the AI message generator.
- **Turso**: For your database.
- **Twilio (Optional)**: If you decide to enable SMS marketing in the future.

## 2. API Keys & Connection
- Open the `.env` file in the root directory.
- Replace the following with your own production keys:
  - `STRIPE_SECRET_KEY` & `STRIPE_PUBLISHABLE_KEY`
  - `STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET` (Generate this in Stripe Dashboard under Developers > Webhooks)
  - `RESEND_API_KEY`
  - `OPENAI_API_KEY`
  - `TURSO_DATABASE_URL` & `TURSO_AUTH_TOKEN`

## 3. Connecting Stripe
- Create the 3 subscription plans (Starter, Growth, Pro) and the Done-for-You Setup in your Stripe Dashboard.
- Copy the Price IDs and update the corresponding variables in your `.env` file.
- Set up a Webhook in Stripe pointing to `https://your-domain.com/api/webhooks/stripe`.

## 4. How to Test the App
- **Sign Up**: Create a test account on your homepage.
- **Checkout**: Select a plan and ensure you are redirected to Stripe.
- **Onboarding**: Complete the onboarding form.
- **QR Sign**: Go to your dashboard and download your unique QR sign.
- **Opt-in**: Scan the QR code and sign up as a "customer" to trigger the email sequence.

## 5. How to Get Your First 10 Customers
- **The "Founder's Offer"**: Offer the $497 Done-for-You setup for free to the first 5 local businesses in your town in exchange for a video testimonial.
- **Walk-ins**: Visit local coffee shops or boutiques. Show them the "VIP Club" QR sign on your phone.
- **LinkedIn Outreach**: Use the "Heartfelt" outreach copy generated by the Marketer to message local business owners.

## 6. What to Post Online
- **Facebook/Instagram**: "Tired of good customers disappearing? We help you build a VIP club that brings them back automatically. DM for a free QR sign."
- **Blog**: Use the SEO templates in the `src/app/blog` directory to build authority.
