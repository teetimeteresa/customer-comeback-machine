# Med Spa Happiness Guide: "The Glow Partner"

*Goal: To move from 'Selling Treatments' to 'Partnering in Confidence.'*

---

## 1. The "Peak Result" Check-in (The 90-Day Loop)
Med spa treatments (Botox, fillers, etc.) have a "peak" and then a gradual fade.
- **Action:** Automated nudge at Day 75 (before they start to notice the fade).
- **Copy:** *"Hi {Name}! Your results should be at their absolute peak right now—I hope you're loving what you see in the mirror! ✨ Just a heads-up that we’re starting to fill up for next month. If you want to keep that glow seamless, now is the perfect time to grab your next 'Refresh' spot."*
- **Happiness Impact:** It feels like a concierge service, not a sales nudge.

---

## 2. The "Post-Treatment Care" Concierge
Many med spa treatments have specific downtime.
- **Action:** Sequence of "Care Tips" sent via SMS/Email.
- **Hour 1:** Ice instructions + "You did great!"
- **Day 1:** What to expect (redness, etc.) + "It's totally normal!"
- **Day 3:** "The glow is coming!"
- **Happiness Impact:** Reduces "buyer's remorse" and post-procedure anxiety.

---

## 3. The "Founding Member" Community
For a new Med Spa owner, the first 100 clients are their lifeblood.
- **Action:** Invite clients to the "Founding Glow Club."
- **Benefit:** Exclusive access to new treatments before they launch + a personal thank-you note from the owner.

---

## 4. Surprise & Delight: The "Self-Care" Shoutout
- **Action:** Automated message on a random Tuesday.
- **Copy:** *"Hi {Name}, just a random reminder from {Business Name} that you deserve a little self-care today. Even if it's just 5 minutes of quiet. You're doing great! ❤️"*
- **Happiness Impact:** Builds deep emotional brand loyalty that survives price wars.

---

## 5. ROI Hooks for Sales (The Happiness Angle)
- **Protecting the CAC:** You spend $300 to get them in the door. If they don't come back for their refresh, you lose money. Happiness automation *protects* that $300 investment.
- **Review Generation:** Med Spas live and die by photos and reviews. Our automation asks for the review when they are "Feeling the Glow" (Day 10 post-treatment), ensuring the highest possible rating.
