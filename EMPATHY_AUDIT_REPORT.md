# ❤️ Empathy Audit & Mystery Shopper Report

**Date:** May 28, 2026
**Auditor:** Happiness Lead

## 1. Landing Page Experience (The First Impression)
*   **Tone:** The tone is excellent. Phrases like *"Because every customer is a neighbor"* and *"heart-centered follow-up system"* immediately resonate with our target audience of local business owners.
*   **Design:** The use of warm colors (Amber) and high-quality UI design (from the code review) feels trustworthy and professional without being corporate.
*   **Friction:** The free tool is currently hard to find from the landing page. A mystery shopper would expect a direct CTA for the tool in the hero section or top nav.

## 2. Free Tool Experience (/free-tool)
*   **Flow:** The step-by-step questionnaire is simple and easy to understand.
*   **Empathy:** The tool asks about "Brand Tone," which shows we understand that every business has a unique voice.
*   **Friction:** Currently blocked by a technical 500 error (ENOENT). This is a critical "Hurt" point for a new visitor.
*   **Content:** The generated messages in `data.ts` are solid. I suggest adding a "Neighborly" category that specifically uses the language of community.

## 3. Onboarding Experience
*   **Tone:** The onboarding form is thorough.
*   **Friction:** The transition from "Paid" to "Onboarding" is where we often lose people (based on our 11 active subs vs 866 leads). We should ensure the "Welcome" message is immediate and warm.
*   **Surprise & Delight:** We've already implemented the "Virtual Coffee" and "Happiness Tip," which are brilliant touches.

## 🚀 Recommended Happiness Boosts:
1.  **Direct CTA for Free Tool:** Add a prominent button in the Hero section: *"Try our Free Follow-Up Generator"*
2.  **Neighborly Tone Option:** Add a new tone category to the free tool called "Neighborly" (Refined versions of Friendly/Heartfelt).
3.  **Error Recovery:** If the tool fails, show a "Neighborly Oops" page that offers a manual response instead of a raw 500 error.
4.  **Blog Integration:** Add a "Free Tool" banner at the end of every blog post.

**Status:** The soul of CCM is strong, but the "Machine" needs a little oil to keep the neighborly feelings flowing smoothly. ❤️🥂
