# Public Promotion Areas for Customer Comeback Machine

## 1. Reddit Subreddits
*   **r/smallbusiness:** Focus on "How I automated my review requests" or "The economics of retention" value-add posts.
*   **r/entrepreneur:** Share the "Founder Story" and the "Set It and Forget It" philosophy.
*   **r/localbusiness:** Post tips on competing with big-box retailers using automation.
*   **r/marketing:** Discuss the "70% vanishing customer" statistic and the solution.

## 2. Online Communities
*   **Indie Hackers:** Post a product launch update and share the growth metrics of the first 10 customers.
*   **Product Hunt:** Schedule a launch once the "Done-for-You" offer is fully polished.
*   **Facebook Groups:** Target "Local Business Owners" and "Main Street Retailers" groups.

## 3. Niche Forums
*   **SalonGeek:** Promoting the "Review Growth" angle specifically for salons.
*   **The Coffee Forum:** Focusing on "Loyalty on Autopilot" for cafes.
*   **Retail Dive / RetailWire (Comments/Networking):** Engaging with retail tech discussions.

## 4. Local Asheville/Charleston Networking
*   **CreativeMornings (Charleston/Asheville chapters):** Networking with local creative business owners.
*   **1 Million Cups:** Presenting the CCM solution to the local entrepreneur community.
