# 📖 The Storyteller Approach: DFY Email Templates
These templates are designed for the **Done-for-You ($497)** tier. The goal is to move from "automated bot" to "trusted local partner" by weaving in the owner's unique story.

---

## The Formula
Every storyteller email should have:
1. **The Hook:** A personal detail from the onboarding form (Why they started, a favorite product, or a specific brand word).
2. **The Connection:** Linking that detail to the customer's experience.
3. **The "Heart-Centered" CTA:** Inviting them back as a neighbor, not just a transaction.

---

## Template 1: The Warm Welcome (Immediately after opt-in)
**Subject:** So glad we met today! (and a little secret...) ❤️

Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you so much for stopping by [Business Name] today. 

When I first started this [Business Type], I did it because [Insert "Why" from Onboarding - e.g., "I couldn't find a decent croissant in this town"]. Seeing people like you enjoy [Main Product] is exactly why we keep the lights on.

I'm so glad you're part of our inner circle now. Keep an eye out for a little surprise on your birthday!

Warmly,
[Owner Name]
[Business Name]

---

## Template 2: The "Favor" (2 days later - Review Request)
**Subject:** A quick question from [Owner Name]...

Hi [Customer Name],

It's [Owner Name] here again. I hope you're still smiling after your visit!

As a small local business, we don't have a giant marketing budget. We rely on "digital handshakes"—which is what we call Google reviews.

[Insert "Brand Tone" detail - e.g., "We like to keep things simple and heartfelt here."]

If you have 30 seconds, could you share what you loved about your visit? It helps other neighbors find us.

[Button: Leave a Review]

Deeply appreciated,
[Owner Name]

---

## Template 3: The "Comeback" (7 days later)
**Subject:** Thinking of you (and your favorite [Product])

Hi [Customer Name],

It's been a week since we saw you, and I wanted to reach out. 

[Insert "Special Word" or "Favorite Phrase" - e.g., "We believe every visit should feel like a mini-vacation."]

To make your next "mini-vacation" even better, I'd love to offer you [Offer - e.g., 10% off your next visit]. Just show this email at the counter!

Can't wait to see you again.

Best,
[Owner Name]

## Template 4: The "Referral" (21 days later)
**Subject:** Know someone who loves [Product] as much as you?

Hi [Customer Name],

At [Business Name], we've always been about community. In fact, [Insert Detail - e.g., "most of our regular customers started as friends of friends"].

If you have a friend who would love [What Makes Business Special], we'd love to meet them!

If you send them our way, we'll give them [Referral Offer] and add a little something special to your account too.

Building this community together,
[Owner Name]

---

## Template 5: The "Win-Back" (45 days later)
**Subject:** We miss your face! ❤️

Hi [Customer Name],

I was just looking through our customer list and noticed it's been a while since we saw you at [Business Name].

I hope everything is wonderful in your world! We've been busy [Insert Recent Update or Season - e.g., "roasting a new batch of Ethiopian beans"], and it's just not the same without our regulars.

Whenever you're ready to stop back in, your first [Product] is on me. 

See you soon?
[Owner Name]
