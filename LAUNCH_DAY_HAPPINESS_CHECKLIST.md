# 🎈 Your Launch Day Happiness Checklist

Congratulations! Your **Customer Comeback Machine** is live. Today isn't just about software—it's about the first few steps toward building a deeper connection with your community.

Here is your simple, 5-minute checklist to ensure today is a success.

---

## 1. The "First Scan" Celebration
*   [ ] **Test it yourself:** Scan the QR code on your counter. Experience exactly what your customers see. When that first "Thank You" email hits your inbox, take a second to smile—your business is now officially more connected.
*   [ ] **Celebrate with your team:** Show your staff the new QR sign. Explain that this is our new way of saying "thank you" even after the customer leaves.

## 2. Setting the Scene
*   [ ] **Location, Location, Location:** Place your QR sign where people naturally wait—at the register, on the tables, or near the pickup station.
*   [ ] **The "Personal Nudge":** When you see a regular customer scan it, give them a wink or a quick "Thanks for joining our list, {Name}!" It makes the digital connection feel real.

## 3. Reviewing Your "First 5"
*   [ ] **Check your dashboard:** At the end of the day, peek at your "Total Opt-ins." Even if it’s just 1 or 2 people, those are 1 or 2 neighbors who are now part of your inner circle.
*   [ ] **Read your first response:** When your first review request goes out (in 2 days), keep an eye on your Google Business Profile. Respond to that first review with a personal "Thank you so much!"

## 4. Spread the Love Online
*   [ ] **Social Media Shoutout:** Post a photo of your new QR sign on Instagram or Facebook. Use the caption: *"We've officially launched our VIP Club! Scan this next time you're in for a special birthday treat and a big thank-you from our family to yours. ❤️"*
