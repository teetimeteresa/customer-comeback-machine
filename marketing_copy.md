## 3. Automated Email Templates (Default Sequence)

### Email 1: Immediate Thank-You
*   **Trigger:** Immediately after opt-in
*   **Subject:** Thank you for stopping in!
*   **Body:**
    Hi [First Name],

    It was so great to see you at [Business Name] today! We know you have plenty of choices, and we’re truly grateful you chose to spend your time and support with us.

    If there is anything we can do to make your next visit even better, please let us know.

    Warmly,
    The team at [Business Name]

### Email 2: Review Request
*   **Trigger:** 2 days after opt-in
*   **Subject:** Could you do us a quick favor?
*   **Body:**
    Hi [First Name],

    We hope you’re still enjoying your recent visit to [Business Name]!

    As a local business, word-of-mouth means the world to us. Would you mind taking 30 seconds to share your experience? Your feedback helps us grow and helps other neighbors find us.

    [Link: Leave a Review on Google]

    Thank you so much for your support!

    Best,
    [Business Name]

## 4. Free Tool Copy: “What Follow-Up Should I Send My Customer?”

### Generator Questions:
1.  **What kind of business do you run?** (Options: Boutique, Salon, Coffee Shop, Bakery, Med Spa, Photographer, Fitness Studio, Other)
2.  **What did the customer do?** (Options: Bought a product, Booked a service, Just browsed)
3.  **What’s your brand tone?** (Options: Friendly, Professional, Luxury, Funny, Heartfelt, Simple)
4.  **What’s your main goal?** (Options: Get a review, Bring them back, Ask for a referral, Get a birthday list, Thank-you message)

### Example Generation Logic (Boutique + Bought + Friendly + Get a review):
*   **1 Thank-You Message:** "Hey [Name]! Thanks for grabbing that new piece today. Can't wait to see how you style it!"
*   **1 Review Request:** "We're so glad you found something you love! Would you mind sharing the love with a quick Google review?"
*   **1 Comeback Offer:** "Already thinking about your next look? Use code COMEBACK for 10% off your next visit!"
*   **1 Referral Message:** "Friends don't let friends miss out on great finds. Refer a friend and you both get a treat!"
*   **1 Social Media Caption:** "Nothing makes our day like seeing a happy customer find their new favorite thing. ✨ #ShopLocal"
